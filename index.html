About Me

Nicholas "Nick" DiNardo is a dynamic Senior Advisor to the Under Secretary of State for Public Diplomacy, where he leads efforts to strengthen the relationships between the people of the United States and citizens of other countries. With a keen focus on integrating AI responsibly across Public Diplomacy sections at U.S. Embassies and Consulates worldwide, Nick ensures that innovative technologies enhance engagement and understanding with foreign audiences. He also plays a pivotal role in managing the Under Secretary's public image and outreach strategy, advising on critical foreign policy issues, and overseeing support staff operations.

Despite his young age, Nick has swiftly ascended to a senior rank, demonstrating exceptional acumen and skill in his field. He is committed to continuous self-improvement and proving his worth, aspiring to eventually run his own PR firm or become a senior executive at a respected firm in Washington, DC, or New York.

Nick's interests are as diverse as his professional responsibilities. An avid rugby enthusiast and video game aficionado, he balances his high-stakes career with his passions. He is deeply invested in travel, pop culture, and exploring new destinations, always seeking enriching experiences that broaden his perspective.

As a bisexual man, Nick is particularly attuned to how the U.S. Government engages with the LGBTQIA community. He values inclusive dialogue and innovative outreach strategies that bring diverse voices into the fold. In his personal life, Nick cherishes his relationship with his boyfriend, Barton Liang, navigating the complexities of an open relationship with communication and mutual respect.

Nick's vibrant personality, professional dedication, and personal experiences make him a compelling figure in the realm of public diplomacy, continually striving to make a positive impact on a global scale.
